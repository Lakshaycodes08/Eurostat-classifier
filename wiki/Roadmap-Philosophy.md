# Roadmap & Philosophy

## Philosophy

- **Explicit over implicit** – No hidden execution paths.
- **Deterministic over probabilistic** – Same inputs, same behavior.
- **Contracts over prompts** – tooling.json and Wrekenfiles are the contract; agents don't interpret policy.

## Direction

- Stable CLI and clear docs (Pages + Wiki).
- Cursor, Claude, and MCP as first-class integration paths.
- Security and determinism remain non-negotiable; no "agent autonomy" over execution.
