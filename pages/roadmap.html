Roadmap

Direction and philosophy. For detailed backlog and design rationale, see Wiki → Roadmap & Philosophy.

Principles

  • Explicit over implicit – No hidden execution paths.
  • Deterministic over probabilistic – Same inputs, same behavior.
  • Contracts over prompts – tooling.json and Wrekenfiles are the contract; agents don’t interpret policy.

Current focus

Stable CLI, Cursor/Claude/MCP integrations, and clear docs. Security and determinism stay non-negotiable.
